# Gen 1 Species Summary -- All 151 Profiles

Consolidated per-species collector profiles for all 151 Gen 1 Pokemon, assembled from the 14 game-specific research documents ([01-vc-red-blue.md](01-vc-red-blue.md) through [14-gen9-sv.md](14-gen9-sv.md)). See [00-overview.md](00-overview.md) for the species index, tier definitions, transfer chain reference, and terminology.

**Document scope:** For each species, this document identifies the distinct specimen targets a completionist collector should pursue, the minimum set covering all irreplaceable distinctions, mutual exclusions, impossible combinations, recommended nature, legacy moves worth noting, and suggested acquisition order across games.

**Tier treatment:**
- **Tier 1** (starters, fossils, legendaries, mythicals, gifts, Game Corner): Full profiles with detailed specimen tables
- **Tier 2** (regional forms, Safari Zone, version exclusives, trade evolutions): Medium profiles with form coverage
- **Tier 3** (common wild encounters): Brief profiles focused on key specimen targets

## #001 Bulbasaur

**Type:** Grass/Poison | **Gender:** 87.5M | **Egg Groups:** Monster, Grass | **Hidden Ability:** Chlorophyll

### Forms
- Kanto (base) only. No regional forms.
- Venusaur has Mega Evolution (Venusaurite, Grass/Poison, Thick Fat) and Gigantamax (G-Max Vine Lash).

### Specimen Targets
| Target | Category | Source Game | Ball | Shiny? | Role | Notes |
|--------|----------|------------|------|--------|------|-------|
| VC Yellow gift | Mandatory | VC Yellow | Poke Ball | Optional | Origin trophy | Game Boy mark + HA Chlorophyll. Yellow gives all 3 starters on one save. |
| HGSS bred | Mandatory | HGSS | Apricorn Ball (Friend Ball) | Optional | Ball specimen | Earliest legal Apricorn Ball Bulbasaur. Breed from traded/transferred parent. |
| SV Terarium catch | Optional | Scarlet/Violet (Indigo Disk) | Any modern ball | Yes | Shiny + mark hunt | Wild in Terarium; eligible for marks. Best modern shiny source. |
| LGPE catch | Optional | Let's Go P/E | Premier Ball | Yes | Shiny specimen | Gift from Cerulean City NPC (high stats); catch combo shiny elsewhere. |
| Friend Safari (XY) | Optional | Pokemon X/Y | Any ball | Yes | HA source | Ivysaur in Grass Friend Safari -- easiest HA source in Gen 6. |

### Minimum Collector Set
1. **VC Yellow** -- Game Boy origin, HA Chlorophyll, Poke Ball (irreplaceable origin)
2. **HGSS Apricorn Ball** -- Friend Ball bred specimen (irreplaceable ball legality)
3. **Modern shiny** -- SV Masuda Method or Terarium wild (shiny in any preferred ball)

### Maximum Collector Set
Add: VC shiny (male only, 1/8192), LGPE shiny, Friend Safari HA specimen, Beast Ball bred (Gen 7+), Gmax Venusaur (SwSh Max Soup), Mega Venusaur-ready specimen (Gen 6).

### Mutual Exclusions
- **VC origin:** Shiny OR female, never both (87.5M species, Attack DV conflict).

### Impossible Combinations
- Shiny + female from VC origin (Gen 1/2 DV system makes Attack DV 2+ required for shiny, but female requires 0-1).
- Apricorn Ball + Game Boy mark (VC balls convert to Poke Ball on transfer; HGSS Apricorn Balls have no origin mark).

### Best Nature
**Modest** (Venusaur is primarily a special attacker; +SpA -Atk). Timid viable for speed-oriented sets. Bold for defensive/Mega Venusaur tank.

### Legacy Moves Worth Preserving
- VC moves ARE preserved through Poke Transporter transfer. Gen 1 TMs and Gen 2 tutor moves (e.g., Crystal's Flamethrower/Ice Beam/Thunderbolt) create legal transfer-only specimens in Gen 7+. Check species-specific TM/tutor compatibility for transfer value. Gen 3+ tutor/TM moves are broadly re-learnable in modern games.

### Acquisition Order
1. VC Yellow (Game Boy origin + all starters on one save)
2. HGSS (Apricorn Ball breeding)
3. Gen 6 Friend Safari or SV Terarium (HA, shiny hunting)
4. SwSh (Gmax factor via Max Soup if desired)

## #002 Ivysaur

**Type:** Grass/Poison | **Gender:** 87.5M | **Egg Groups:** Monster, Grass | **Hidden Ability:** Chlorophyll

Middle evolution. Collector profile is subsumed by Bulbasaur (breed/catch) and Venusaur (final form). An Ivysaur specimen is only needed if collecting all three stages separately.

**Modest** (same as Bulbasaur line). Eviolite set uses Bold.

### Forms

| HGSS Bill's Eevee | Mandatory | HGSS | Poke Ball (gift) | No | Base | Goldenrod City gift from Bill. Breed in Apricorn Ball for ball specimens. |

### Minimum Collector Set
1. **VC RB** -- Game Boy origin, HA Anticipation
2. **HGSS Apricorn Ball bred** -- Moon Ball or Love Ball
3. **Modern shiny** -- SV or Gen 6

Add: VC shiny (male only), LGPE partner Eevee (shiny locked), Beast Ball bred, Gmax Eevee (SwSh), one shiny per Eeveelution in matching balls (Vaporeon/Dive, Jolteon/Quick, Flareon/Repeat, etc.).

### Impossible Combinations
- Shiny + female from VC origin.
- Shiny partner Eevee from LGPE.

### Best Nature
Depends entirely on target Eeveelution. Eevee itself: **Jolly** or **Adamant** if keeping as Eevee. For breeding stock: nature is irrelevant (use Everstone to pass desired nature to offspring).

### Acquisition Order
1. VC Red/Blue (origin trophy)
2. HGSS (Apricorn Ball breeding, one per eeveelution target)
3. B2W2 or XY Friend Safari (HA)
4. SwSh (Gmax)
